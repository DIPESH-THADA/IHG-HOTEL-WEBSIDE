Make the Most of Your Trip
One Trip, Two Cities
Discover the perfect combination of thrill and chill by exploring two cities in one unforgettable trip. Immerse yourself in the heart of the city, where you can delight in the energy of the downtown and explore its nightlife. Then set off for the quiet, peaceful nature of coastal towns or mountainside retreats.
Baja California Sur, Mexico
La Paz and Todos Santos
Discover the magic of Baja California SurBaja California Sur by pairing two distinct destinations in one unforgettable journey. Start in La Paz, a vibrant seaside city known for its walkable malecón, incredible marine life, and lively culture. Then slow down in Todos Santos, a tranquil hideaway nestled between the desert and the Pacific. This combination of adventure and relaxation captures the best of Baja. If you have some extra time, check out nearby Cabo San Lucas making it an ideal multicity getaway.
Germany
Berlin and Hamburg
Take in Berlin’s rebellious creativity and Hamburg’s maritime elegance on a culture-packed northern Germany escape. Tour Berlin’s street art, museums, and techno clubs, then hop a quick train ride to Hamburg’s historic port, cutting-edge architecture, and famous nightlife.
Saudi Arabia
Riyadh and Jeddah
Experience two vibrant sides of Saudi Arabia on a single journey. In Riyadh, tradition meets transformation—explore ancient souks, a burgeoning art scene, and breathtaking desert landscapes in the heart of Saudi Arabia. Then head to Jeddah, where the charm of the Red Sea blends with history and modernity—stroll along the historic Al-Balad district, enjoy world-class dining, and soak in the coastal city's laid-back yet cosmopolitan vibe.
Texas, USA
Austin and San Antonio
Take a road trip through Central Texas and experience two cities that celebrate culture, music, and flavor. In Austin, enjoy live bands, funky food trucks, and lakeside lounging. Then head south to San Antonio for historic missions, riverfront strolls, and Tex-Mex classics in a slower-paced, heritage-rich setting.
